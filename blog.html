How does

Face mask detecion work?

Face mask detection is an AI based technology that analyzes a video stream to detect and recognize a face mask worn by an individual person. Every individual is classified either as ‘wearing a mask’ or flagged as ‘not wearing a mask’. If the face mask detector application identifies a user as not wearing a mask, a custom message can be delivered via a digital screen to remind all visitors to wear masks before entering the premises.

How to

Wear mask correctly

According to the guidelines set by the World Health Organization (WHO) a face mask needs to cover the face fully, including the nose and the chin. Therefore, the detector only classifies someone as wearing a mask if these conditions are satisfied. software is also spoof proof which means that it understands if you’re covering your face with a hand or an object other than a mask.
